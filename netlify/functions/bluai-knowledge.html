Personalization and Design File Requirements Title: Personalization and Design File Requirements We accept high-resolution files in PNG, JPG, JPEG, SVG, or PDF format. Every detail matters, so files must be clear, high-quality, and not blurry. We use a standard CMYK color profile. For the best print results, files should have a minimum of 300 DPI. Avoid screenshots or small, pixelated images. If sending designs via WhatsApp, please send the file as a document, not a photo, to maintain full quality. You must select your preferred print size before uploading your design. For Tees, sizes range from A5 Mini (5" x 8") to A2 Max (16" x 23"). For Totes, the print size is A4 Mid (8" x 11"). You can upload at least three files per order, with each file under 10MB. If you have a specific design placement (front, center, corner), please let us know by adding a note during checkout or uploading a reference image. Clear file naming (e.g., "front tote image.png") is essential for accurate placement. Once an order is placed and the design file is confirmed, changes cannot be made after a strict 6-hour window, as our production process begins shortly thereafter. We cannot guarantee exact color matches due to variations in monitor settings and material absorption rates. If you are unsure about your file quality, please reach out to us for assistance via our Contact Form, WhatsApp, Call, SMS at +234 708 005 4074, or Email at hello@iamxis.com.ng. III. Product Care and Durability Title: Care Instructions and Product Durability All of our prints are crisp, durable, and made to last with proper care. Our personalized Tees are made from 100% breathable cotton and should be washed inside out in cold water and hung dry. Avoid using bleach and do not iron directly over the custom print. Totes are crafted from 100% durable cotton and should be spot-cleaned gently with mild detergent and a soft cloth. Avoid machine washing or scrubbing totes. Both Glossy Mugs and Magic Mugs are high-quality, but for maximum print longevity, we recommend hand washing them with warm water and mild soap. Avoid using dishwashers for mugs. Totes are designed for everyday carry; select totes include a lined interior with pockets for added functionality. We offer custom made options which can be viewed in our Custom Made Edit section here - https://iamxis.com.ng/collections/custom-made/. For any special requests related to custom orders, please reach out to our support team directly. IV. Sizing Guide Title: Detailed Sizing Information Tees: Available in sizes S through 3XL.
